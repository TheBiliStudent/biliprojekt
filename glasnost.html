Glasnost

It was the guiding principle of a political reformation supporting the policy of ="perestroika.html">"Perestroika" that aimed at with the aims of increasing transparency of the government and its action and granting the freedom of speech to criticize wrongs within the socialist system.

Soon enough, the new transparency revealed shocking lies the Communist Party had hidden before, such as ecological problems that were labeled as problems only capitalism caused, and a liberalized political discourse was used to not only talk about wrongs
within the system, but how wrong the system itself was putting even more pressure on the government.
