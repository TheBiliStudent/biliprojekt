Transformation Theory

Transformation Theory describe the process of a system such as a political or economic transitioning into another.

To have an ideal typical transformation, there have to be three criteria met.

Liberalization

The old system destroyed; the people are “liberated” from it.

Democratization

The system is transformed, a new system is established.

Consolidation

The political actors make sure that the new system does not break apart again.
